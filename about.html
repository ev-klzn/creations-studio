Creations Studio:
Crafting Unique & Sustainable Spaces Since 2008

At Creations Studio, our mission is to design unique and comfortable living and working spaces that harmoniously blend aesthetics and functionality, while prioritizing an individual approach for each client.

We are committed to utilizing environmentally friendly materials and innovative technologies to enhance the quality of life for our customers and create sustainable environments.

We Specialize In

  • Architecture

    Architectural design is vital for any construction and renovation, important for residential and commercial spaces. In our company, specialists take the following features into account when designing a house:

    • Functionality and Aesthetics: We focus on creating layouts that meet the residents' needs while ensuring an attractive design that reflects modern architectural trends.
    • Sustainability and Compliance: Our projects prioritize energy efficiency through eco-friendly materials and technologies, while adhering to local building codes and regulations for safety and legality.
    • Community Integration and Safety: We design homes that harmoniously fit into their surroundings and consider the needs of diverse populations, ensuring accessibility and safety for all residents.
    Book consultation

  • Interior Design

    Interior design is a very important stage in the work on creating a design that will make your life more comfortable, brighter and happier.

    The interior design of an apartment, house, cottage or office takes into account the following important features:

    • Functionality and Flow: We design layouts that optimize space usage and ensure easy movement, making every area effective for its intended purpose.
    • Aesthetic Appeal and Personalization: Our designs combine style and visual harmony, tailored to reflect the unique tastes and preferences of the occupants.
    • Comfort and Sustainability: We prioritize elements that enhance well-being, such as lighting and ergonomics, while using eco-friendly materials to promote a healthier environment.
    Book consultation

  • Furniture & Decor

    The choice of furniture and decor is a crucial element of interior design, playing a decisive role in shaping the overall atmosphere of the space. At this stage, our company's specialists work closely with customers to select the best furniture and decor elements that align with the chosen style and vision. Our team emphasizes:

    • Quality and Durability: We prioritize selecting high-quality materials and finishes that not only enhance the aesthetic but also stand the test of time.
    • Color and Theme Coordination: Careful attention is given to ensure that all elements complement the overall color palette and thematic direction of the project.
    • Functional Design: We focus on furniture that is not only visually appealing but also functional, ensuring it meets the practical needs of the space and its occupants.
